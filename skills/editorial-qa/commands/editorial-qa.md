# /editorial-qa

Review and improve marketing content. Context: $ARGUMENTS

## Steps

1. **Identify the content** -- Is it pasted inline, in a file, or described? Read it first.
2. **Determine context** -- What client? What channel? What audience? What's the goal?
3. **Run the review** -- Score all 6 dimensions from the SKILL.md:
   - Clarity & Structure
   - Tone & Voice
   - CTA Quality
   - SEO Alignment
   - Technical Quality
   - Persuasion & Engagement
4. **Produce the report** -- Issues table with severity, location, and specific fixes
5. **Write the revision** -- Full revised version with changes in bold
6. **SEO check** -- Keyword placement, meta description, internal links

## If content is a draft in progress

Focus feedback on structure and direction rather than polish. Flag structural issues first, save grammar for final pass.

## If reviewing for a specific client

Check if brand voice guidelines exist (Notion, shared docs). Apply them. If none exist, suggest establishing them.
