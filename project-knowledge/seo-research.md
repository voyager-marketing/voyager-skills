# SEO Research

Use this to research SEO data for a client domain or topic. Triggers on: "SEO research for", "keyword research", "check rankings for", "backlink analysis", "competitor SEO", "content gap analysis", "what keywords should we target", "SEO audit", "how is [domain] doing in search".

## Available Ahrefs Tools

### Keyword Research
- Keywords Explorer Overview -- volume, difficulty, CPC
- Matching Terms -- keywords containing a term
- Related Terms -- semantically related keywords
- Search Suggestions -- long-tail autocomplete ideas
- Volume History -- trending vs declining

### Site Analysis
- Site Explorer Metrics -- DR, organic traffic, backlinks overview
- Organic Keywords -- what the site ranks for
- Top Pages -- pages driving the most organic traffic
- Organic Competitors -- who competes in SERPs
- Domain Rating + history

### Backlinks
- Referring Domains -- who links to this site
- All Backlinks -- full list
- Broken Backlinks -- reclaim opportunities
- Anchors -- anchor text distribution

### Google Search Console (if connected)
- Keyword performance, page performance, traffic trends, ranking history

## Research Workflows

### Quick Domain Health Check
1. DR, traffic, backlinks overview
2. Top 20 organic keywords by traffic
3. Recent performance trend

### Keyword Research for Content
1. Seed keywords from client niche
2. Volume + difficulty for each
3. Expand with matching/related terms and suggestions
4. Check existing rankings for quick wins
5. Prioritize: high volume + low KD + business relevance

### Competitor Gap Analysis
1. Identify top 3-5 organic competitors
2. Pull each competitor's top keywords by traffic
3. Find keywords where competitors rank but client doesn't

### Backlink Opportunities
1. Client's referring domains vs top competitor's
2. Who links to competitor but not client?
3. Broken backlinks for easy reclaims

### Recommendations

### Data Sources
- Tools used, date range, approximate API credits

## Rules

- Always include data with every recommendation
- Note the date of all data pulls
- Don't make claims without data -- say "data unavailable" if needed
- Tailor recommendations to the client's business, not generic SEO advice
- Be conservative with Ahrefs API credits
