# Editorial QA

Use this to revise, proofread, voice-edit, or quality-check any marketing content. Triggers on: "review this copy", "edit this", "proofread", "tighten this up", "check the tone", "is this on brand", "editorial review", "QA this content", "make this better", "punch this up".

## Review Dimensions

### 1. Clarity & Structure
- Is the main point obvious in the first 2 sentences?
- Does each section earn its place?
- Are transitions smooth and logical?
- Is the reading level appropriate for the audience?

### 2. Tone & Voice
- Does it match the client's brand voice (if guidelines exist)?
- Does it match Voyager's voice when writing for the agency?
- Is the tone consistent throughout?
- Confident without arrogant, helpful without condescending?

### 3. CTA Quality
- Is there a clear, single primary CTA?
- Is the CTA specific? ("Schedule a 15-min call" beats "Contact us")
- Does the content build toward the CTA naturally?

### 4. SEO Alignment
- Focus keyword in: title, H1, first paragraph, meta description?
- Secondary keywords woven naturally (not stuffed)?
- Meta description compelling and under 160 chars?
- Internal links present and relevant?

### 5. Technical Quality
- Grammar, spelling, punctuation
- Consistent formatting (heading hierarchy, list style)
- No orphaned links, broken references, or placeholder text

### 6. Persuasion & Engagement
- Does it open with a hook? (Question, stat, pain point, story)
- Is social proof present where appropriate?
- Are objections anticipated and addressed?

## Output Format

### Overall Grade: A/B/C/D

### What's Working
- Specific callouts of strong elements

### Issues Found
| # | Severity | Location | Issue | Fix |

### Revised Version
Full revised text with changes marked in **bold**.

### SEO Check
- Focus keyword placement, meta description, internal links

## Rules

- Always provide specific rewrites, not just "make it better"
- Preserve the author's voice -- edit, don't rewrite from scratch
- If no brand guidelines exist, default to Voyager voice (direct, warm, no fluff)
- Flag factual claims that need verification
- Be honest about quality
